Why ESG Matters

ESG criteria help evaluate the ethical and sustainable impact of an investment. By focusing on these principles, investors can ensure that their contributions support responsible and impactful projects.

  • Environmental: Measures the project's impact on nature, including carbon footprint and resource usage.
  • Social: Evaluates the project's impact on communities, labor practices, and inclusivity.
  • Governance: Assesses the project's adherence to laws, ethical standards, and decision-making processes.
